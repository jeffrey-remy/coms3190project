My name is Ryan Meyer, and I’m currently a junior in the Software Engineering program at Iowa State University. While I don’t have experience with website design outside of class, I’m thoroughly enjoying the course and have developed a strong interest in web development. I’m also passionate about music and have always appreciated the sense of discovery and community found in old record stores, much like some subreddits today.
